Project

StudyOn| Designer and Front End Developer

Trojan Hackathon | Los Angeles

Fall 2014

StudyOn is an app that aims to enhances study groups' interactions and communications outside classroom. Users can find ongoing study group around campus. It consists of a webapp and a mobile app and we initialized app in the 28 hours TrojanHack.The webapp is well connected the mobile app and data can be transmitted between the two. I came up with the concept and was responsible for the front end of the webapp. The app won the second place in the 2014 USC TrojanHack. We used Foundation, Parse as well as Facebook Plugins in project.
